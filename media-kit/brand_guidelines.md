# Wellvo Brand Guidelines

## Our Mission
**"One tap. Total peace of mind."**

Wellvo is a daily check-in app designed to give families peace of mind. We are the opposite of a medical alert device or a surveillance tool. Our brand should always feel safe, simple, and family-oriented.

---

## 1. Logo & Icon

The core of our visual identity is the Wellvo Icon—a soft, approachable white heart containing a bold checkmark. The heart represents family and care, while the checkmark signifies that "everything is OK."

### 1.1 The Icon (`Icon.svg`)
The standalone icon is primarily used as the iOS App Icon, social media avatars, and favicon.
- It sits on a "Calm Green" background gradient.
- The heart is pure white (`#FFFFFF`) with a "Calm Green" checkmark carved into the center.

### 1.2 The Primary Logo (`Logo.svg`)
The full logo pairs the Icon with the "Wellvo" wordmark. The wordmark uses a robust, clean sans-serif typeface to invoke reliability.
- **Background Usage:** Use this version on light, off-white, or transparent backgrounds.
- **Wordmark Color:** Deep Slate (`#1E293B`)

### 1.3 The Reversed Logo (`Logo-White.svg`)
This is the same lockup as the primary logo, but the "Wellvo" wordmark is set in white (`#FFFFFF`).
- **Background Usage:** Use this version exclusively on dark backgrounds (e.g., dark mode interfaces, solid dark green, or deep navy).

### 1.4 Clear Space & Sizing
- Always maintain clear space around the logo equal to the width of the inner 'W' path.
- The Icon must never be reproduced at a size smaller than `16x16px` (favicon scale).

## 2. Color Palette

Our colors are deliberately chosen to avoid looking clinical or medical. They reflect a feeling of calm and successful connection.

### Primary Color
- **Name:** Calm Green
- **Hex:** `#2ECC71`
- **Gradient Usage (Icon):**
  - **Start:** `#4AE38C`
  - **End:** `#21AE5E`
- **Application:** Used for the primary check-in button, success states, and the core brand identity.

### Secondary Colors
- **Name:** Deep Slate
- **Hex:** `#1E293B`
- **Application:** Used for primary text, headings, and the primary logo wordmark. Gives a modern, high-contrast crispness compared to pure black.
- **Name:** Off-White
- **Hex:** `#F8FAFC`
- **Application:** Used for standard app backgrounds to keep the interface soft and approachable.

---

## 3. Typography

Wellvo's typography is highly legible, accommodating our diverse user base ranging from teenagers to seniors.

### Primary Typeface: SF Pro / Inter
We use system-native fonts to feel at home on the user's device. On iOS, this is **SF Pro**. On the marketing website, we use **Inter**.
- **Weights used:** Regular (for body copy), Semibold (for buttons), Bold (for the Logo wordmark and primary headers).
- **Styling:** Avoid using italics. Let the thick, high-contrast letterforms speak for themselves.

### Accessibility First
Every textual element must meet high-contrast accessibility standards and scale properly with iOS Dynamic Type. The UI must remain clean—if the user increases the text size, the design must not break.
